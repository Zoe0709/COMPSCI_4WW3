Aga Khan Museum

Address:

77 Wynford Drive, Toronto, Ontario M3C 1K1

Phone:

+1 (416)-646-4677

Open Hours:

Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun: 10:00am - 5:30pm

Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays: Closed

Description:

Aga Khan Museum is a stunning Toronto museum dedicated to celebrating the Islamic Arts. It presents and collects art from historically significant Muslim civilizations as well as contemporary Muslim communities and diasporas around the world.
